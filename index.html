Process

How it works?

Equipped with an IoT pouch with the blood packet inside, Bleedlytix tracks the flow of blood right from it’s donation to end usage through an app designed for custom needs for each of the stakeholders - Donor, NGO, Blood Bank, Hospital, Patient & Health Agency.

Faq

Questions and Answers.

We've put together some commonly asked questions on the services offered by Bleedlytix.

What does the IoT device track?

The reusable IoT blood pouch tracks temperature, quantity of blood, duration, and, GPS co-ordinates of the blood packet.

Where do we store the user info?

All client-specific information is stored on the user's mobile.

What benefits does the blood-donor get?

Donors get a free blood test and priority during hospitalization that enables quicker blood delivery when in need for their close ones.

Do you offer any additional services to a patient?

Patient can request blood through app connecting directly to the donor
