Process

How it works?

Equipped with an IoT pouch with the blood packet inside, Bleedlytix tracks the flow of blood right from it’s donation to end usage through an app designed for custom needs for each of the stakeholders - Donor, NGO, Blood Bank, Hospital, Patient & Health Agency.

Donate

Sign up as a donor for a at-home blood collection with incentives

Connect

Connect Blood Banks to nearby NGOs and Hospitals

TARGETING

Help NGOs to setup targeted blood donation drives

TRACK

Track the status of doanted blood and see the current status

Post

Post your own advertisement when in need for blood

Survey

Analyze the demand-supply chain logistics
